About GameExchange.ca

Are you tired of paying almost $100 for a new video game, playing it for a month or two, then trading it in for $20 at your local video game store? GameExchange.ca alleviates that by allowing users to set up trades with other gamers who live nearby.

Setting up a trade is a simple three-step process:

  1. Click the "Propose New Trade" tab
  2. Enter the title and platform of the game you own and want to trade
  3. Enter the title and platform of the game you want to receive in exchange for the game you own
Once you submit your proposed trade, GameExchange.ca will look for a proposed trade by another gamer who wants the game you own, and owns the game you want. If there is a match for your proposed trade, it will show up in the "Your Matches" tab. If you signed up for notifications, you will also receive an email within minutes that a match has been found so you can get in contact with the other gamer right away.
