What is Video Game Exchange?

Video Game Exchange automatically sets up a trade for you with nearby gamers who own the game you want, and want the game you own. Stop getting ripped off by low trade-in values and get new games for the ones you don't play anymore!

How does it work?

Once you're registered, setting up a trade is a simple three-step process:
  1. Click the "Propose New Trade" tab
  2. Enter the title of the game you own and want to trade away
  3. Enter the title of the game you want to receive in exchange for the game you own
Once you submit your proposed trade, Video Game Exchange will look for a proposed trade by another user who wants the game you own, and owns the game you want. If there is a match, it will show up on the "Matches" page. If you signed up for notifications, you will also receive an email within minutes that a match has been found so you can get in contact with the other user right away.
